SDG 11 in the Philippines

In the Philippines, SDG 11 focuses on making cities and human settlements inclusive, safe, resilient, and sustainable. With rapid urbanization, cities like Manila, Cebu, and Davao face challenges such as affordable housing, traffic congestion, disaster risk, and access to green spaces. Efforts are underway to improve public transport, build disaster-resilient infrastructure, and promote sustainable urban planning.
