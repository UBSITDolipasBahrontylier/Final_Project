Affordable Housing in the Philippines

The Philippines faces a significant affordable housing challenge, with millions of Filipinos living in informal settlements or inadequate housing. Government programs and private sector initiatives aim to provide safe, affordable, and resilient homes, especially in urban areas. Innovative approaches, such as socialized housing and community-driven projects, are helping address the housing gap.
